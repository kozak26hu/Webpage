Roadmap

Phase 1: Launch Completed

Token deployed on Ethereum Mainnet (Oct 7, 2025). Initial Uniswap V2 and Balancer PHANDS/WETH pools created. Original staking dApp launched.

Phase 2a: Security Hardening Completed (July 2026)

Full internal review of the staking contract; issues identified and fixed across three iterations (unrestricted emergency-withdraw, reward-accounting bug, a lock-bypass edge case on top-up deposits). Final staking contract deployed with a dedicated rewards treasury, adjustable stake caps, no emergency-withdraw capability, and an owner-gifted locked-stake mechanism (used to guarantee that contributions raised via the community funding round are locked into liquidity on the contributor's behalf). Unified dApp: Swap, Stake, and Add Liquidity now in a single interface.

Phase 2b: Community Foundation (Q3–Q4 2026)

Onboard the first core holders through a controlled, capped airdrop and referral campaign. Organic liquidity growth via LP-staking incentives. Continued transparent reporting of contract activity.

Phase 3: Stabilization & Renounce (Q1–Q2 2027)

Ownership renounce on the token contract once Phase 2 distributions are complete. Third-party security audit. Liquidity pools targeting $5,000–$50,000. Initial CEX-listing outreach.

Phase 4: Expansion (H2 2027+)

DeFi integrations, broader holder base, potential governance exploration.
